  • Program number: 5964

What Gefen Approval Means

Gefen (גפ"ן — גמישות פדגוגית ניהולית) is the Ministry of Education's framework for giving school principals flexibility to choose educational programs. To enter the Gefen catalog:

  1. The supplier submits a program proposal
  2. The proposal is reviewed for alignment with MoE educational goals
  3. If approved, the program appears in the catalog
  4. Individual schools can then choose to purchase it

Important caveats:

  • Gefen approval does NOT mean the MoE endorses the program content
  • Gefen approval does NOT mean the program has been evaluated for effectiveness
  • The MoE allocates ~₪11,500 per student to schools, and principals choose how to spend it
  • There is no mandatory evaluation or outcome reporting
  • Any registered business (עוסק מורשה, חברה, עמותה) can apply
