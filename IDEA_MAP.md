## 1. THE PHILOSOPHICAL FOUNDATION

The site is the applied arm of a larger intellectual project called **"אלכסון" (Diagonal)** by Zvi Shalem. The theoretical framework has a name: **Gnostocracy** — rule by those claiming privileged, self-justifying knowledge.

### The Core Concept: Gnostocracy vs. Commongroundism

```
GNOSTOCRACY (the disease)          COMMONGROUNDISM (the cure)
─────────────────────────          ──────────────────────────
Privileged knowledge ("gnosis")    Shared reference frame
Self-validating truth claims       Falsifiable propositions
Monocriticism (one-way critique)   Reciprocal critique
Epistemic hierarchy                Epistemic humility
"I know, you don't"               "Let's examine together"
Power justifies claims             Evidence justifies claims
Opacity by design                  Transparency by principle
```

hidden meaning — delivered via **opacity by design**.
