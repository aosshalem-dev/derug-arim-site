דירוג ערים — Ideological-Analytical Lens

Framework extracted from the Dror Israel deep-dive (ניתוח עמותת דרור ישראל ערכית.docx). Every suspect org/program gets analyzed through these 9 chapters.

פרקי ניתוח

כל ארגון חשוד עובר ניתוח ב-9 פרקים:

1. זהות ארגונית, רשת עמותות ומבנה שליטה

Organizational identity, NGO network & control structure

  • Is this a single legal entity or a network of affiliated NGOs/companies?
  • Who controls the network — is power centralized in a few people?
  • Do the same people rotate across roles in different entities?
  • Does the org operate a closed community model (urban kibbutz, communes, etc.)?

מקורות נתונים: GuideStar Israel (guidestar.org.il), Companies registrar (ica.justice.gov.il), NGO registrar

2. ניתוח הנהגה וקשרים פוליטיים

Leadership analysis & political connections

  • Who are the key people (founders, CEO, board)?
  • Do leaders have explicit political affiliations or activism history?
  • Has the org or its leaders been involved in political campaigns?
  • Any SLAPP lawsuits (defamation suits to silence critics)?

מקורות נתונים: LinkedIn, news archives, court records, political party records

3. ניתוח כספי ומקורות מימון

Financial analysis & funding sources

  • What is the annual budget? What % comes from government vs foreign donors?
  • Are there connections to politically-oriented foreign foundations (NIF, Ford Foundation, etc.)?
  • Is emergency/crisis funding used to bypass normal channels?
  • Any financial irregularities, audit findings, or unexplained budget gaps?

מקורות נתונים: GuideStar financials, gov.il open data (takziv.gov.il), NGO Monitor, IRS 990 (for US friends-of orgs), Instrumentl

4. אידיאולוגיה ופדגוגיה — חדירת הפדגוגיה הביקורתית

Ideology & pedagogy — critical pedagogy infiltration

  • Does the org use critical pedagogy (Paulo Freire, bell hooks, etc.)?
  • Is there 'semantic shifting' — using traditional terms (Zionism, Judaism) but loading them with progressive content?
  • Does the pedagogy frame existing society as a system of oppression to be dismantled?
  • Are concepts like 'social justice', 'equity', 'decolonization' central to the curriculum?

מקורות נתונים:

5. תכנים בעייתיים והשפעה על חינוך וערכים

Problematic content & impact on education/values

  • Does the program introduce sexual/gender content to minors without adequate parental consent?
  • Is there content that undermines family structure, national identity, or religious tradition?
  • Are facilitators trained in ideology rather than child psychology?
  • Does the org use national memory/trauma (e.g. Rabin assassination) for political framing?

מקורות נתונים:

6. פרשות, חשדות ואי-סדרים

Scandals, suspicions & irregularities

  • Any media investigations or exposés?
  • Any financial scandals, embezzlement allegations?
  • Has the org silenced critics through legal threats?
  • Any complaints from parents, teachers, or former members?

מקורות נתונים: news archives (ha-makom, makor rishon, channel 14), court records

7. השפעה על מדיניות ציבורית וקשרים עם הממשל

Influence on public policy & government relations

  • Does the org have no-bid contracts with government ministries?
  • Does it position itself as essential during crises (wars, COVID)?
  • Are org alumni embedded in Ministry of Education or local government?
  • Does the org influence curriculum or teacher training at scale?
